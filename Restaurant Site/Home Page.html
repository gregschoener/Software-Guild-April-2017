Joes's Family Restaurant was originally established in 1953 by Joe's father Joe Senior; who was a cook on a Navy vessel during WWII. Upon returning home, he used his GI Bill to begin the resrtaurant. It has been a family owned and operated business every since. The menu has changed a bit over the years however the committemnt to providing excellent food for a good price has remained the hallmark of the business.
